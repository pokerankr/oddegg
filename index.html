Release Notes

v1.1.2 April 2025

🥚 An EGGciting Mega Update!

Something ancient is stirring in the egg fields… Hatch 10 Mega-capable Pokémon and unlock the Mega Bracelet — a rare charm that opens the door to something much, much bigger.

  • Mega Eggs — 1-in-50 chance to appear once the bracelet is unlocked. Up to one million steps, 24-hour community countdown, and a menacing heartbeat that speeds up near hatch
  • Shiny Megas — 1-in-20 odds on Mega Eggs (way better than base shinies)
  • Dynamic Mega pool — only Mega forms of Pokémon already in your Living Dex are eligible
  • Mega Dex tab — track all 98 Mega forms; flip between normal and shiny artwork with ‹ › arrows
  • Mega Bracelet charm — appears in the charm bar with a progress tooltip
v1.0.2 April 2025

Ho-Oh Rework

Clicking Ho-Oh as it flies across the screen now reduces the egg's required steps by 10% — making it noticeably easier to hatch without ever triggering it early. The more trainers who catch it, the closer the egg gets!

  • Ho-Oh now appears on mobile too
  • Flies by roughly every 20–30 minutes per device

More Emojis

  • 7 emojis now available: Say hi!, Teamwork!, Don't quit!, WOW!, You Legend!, Good One!, and Pain
v1.0.1 April 2025

Send Emojis to Other Trainers

You can now send a GIF emoji to any trainer who's currently online! Click the Wi-Fi button in the top bar to see who's around, then tap their name to open the emoji picker. Use the arrows to browse, then hit Send.

  • Received emojis appear in the bottom-right corner of the egg screen with a Reply button
  • 30-second cooldown between sends
  • Anonymous Trainers cannot send emojis

Other Fixes

  • Steps no longer accumulate when your screen is locked or the tab is backgrounded
  • Autoclicker threshold raised to allow fast-clicking mouse users
v1.0.0 April 2025

A note on the Step Leaderboard

Shortly after launch, a bug caused one trainer's broken phone screen to register thousands of phantom taps overnight — resulting in an impossibly large step count on the leaderboard.

We've reset the leaderboard to better reflect real play. Any Pokémon hatched during this time have been kept — consider them a whoopsie gift from us. We've also added protections to prevent this from happening again.
